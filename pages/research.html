Why Mood Tracking Matters

According to the World Health Organization, over 264 million people suffer from depression globally. Mental health awareness is growing, and many people are turning to apps to track their emotions and habits.

Trends in Digital Wellness

  • Use of mood tracking apps has increased by 50% in the past 3 years.
  • Gen Z users are more likely to use self-help apps daily.
  • Habit tracking improves consistency and builds positive routines.

How MoodMate Helps

MoodMate allows users to check in with their mood, record daily activities, and identify emotional patterns over time. This helps improve self-awareness and mental well-being.
